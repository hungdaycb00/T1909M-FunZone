2-Day Admission Tickets Dislayland

SUMMER SEASON 2020

Take an extraordinary journey through Germany’s biggest theme park in two days’ time and discover 15 European countries having lots of fun and plenty excitement.

In the detailed designed themed areas, you can not only marvel at country-typical architecture but also choose from a large selection of traditional dishes.

Numerous attractions and shows for every age guarantee, that your visit to Europa-Park will be an unforgettable experience!

Please also note our ticket instructions:

  • Toddlers up to and including 3 years of age receive free admission to the Dislayland
  • Children (aged 4-11) and senior visitors (aged 60+) will receive a reduced admission price for the Dislayland
  • Guests with special needs receive a discounted special rate for this ticket
  • Make a special day even more memorable: Children can enjoy free admission on their birthday (up to and including 12 years old when presenting a valid passport)
  • You have direct access via the main entrance of Dislayland
  • Choose the perfect day for your visit: Your ticket is valid without an extra charge from 28.03.2020 - 08.11.2020 (Summer season 2019)
  • The second visit must be within six days from the first visit
  • 2-day ticket is not transferable
  • Has something gotten in the way? Not a problem: you can use your ticket for a small additional fee, even after the current season
